About Me!

I've been a fan of this band since my childhood (I remember sitting in my car seat rocking out to "Someone Keeps Moving My Chair" on my dad's old car radio). More recently, I've been getting back into the band by expanding my own taste and discovering new favorites from their various albums.

Below I've listed a few of my favorite albums with my favorite songs from each (because honestly, if you could ask me to name a favorite from anything regarding TMBG, I could not possibly give you one answer).
